Comment and reanalysis of Fric et al. (2020)

Ries lab members Elise Larsen and Vaughn Shirey have examined the data and analytical methods used in Fric et al. (2020) and prepared a technical comment including a partial reanalysis for Ecology Letters.

The data and code for this reanalysis can be found at https://github.com/RiesLabGU/Larsen-Shirey2020_EcoLettersComment

The RMarkdown output for both exploring the data and results from the original study and for this reanalysis are available here:
